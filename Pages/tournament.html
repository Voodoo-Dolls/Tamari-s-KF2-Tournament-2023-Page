TOURNAMENT INFO

This is a tournament for KILLING FLOOR 2 centered around the Controlled Difficulty mod -- a mod which allows for fine tuning of difficulty-specific game variables and obtaining a level of consistency and intensity that drives a team experience simply not available in the vanilla game.


The objective of this tournament is not only to unite the community, but to give players (especially newer ones) the opportunity to learn and grow alongside experienced players in mechanical skill, game knowledge, and confidence in a (friendly) competitive environment where there is no fear of being judged. If nothing else, the primary goal is to have FUN, make friends, and show the world what CD (and our community) has to offer!


This tournament is hosted by Tamari, Forrest Mark X, and the Tamari's KF2 Community and is (hopefully) one of many to come. We hope everyone who plays comes out on the otehr end with a whole lot of game knowledge and maybe even some new friends to go with it!

WHO CAN PLAY?

Anyone can sign up as long as they meet the following criteria:


  • At least 300 Hours of Killing Floor 2 game time on record
  • STEAM Account in good standing, free of VAC Bans, Game Bans, and other account restrictions
  • Basic level of fluency in English is a must
  • Must display good mannerisms, excellent sportsmanship, a willingness to be communicative, and a commitment to preserving a fun and toxic-free environment

You don't need to be an expert-level Hell on Earth player to participate in this tournament. Players of ALL skill levels and playstyles, veteran and newbies alike, are welcome AND encouraged to join. All that we ask is that you come forward with an open mind, a positive attitude, and a willingness to learn and expand your knowledge of the game.


You will be placed on a team with several experienced players present as well as a mentor who has volunteered to help show you the ropes and work with you to ensure a fun and fulfilling experience. This is the central purpose and stated objective of the Tamari's KF2 community - to provide new players a means to get into and enjoy CD, free of toxicity, drama, and judgement.


If you're feeling a bit hesitant, that's okay. Just know that you won't be alone and everyone around you will do what they can to make you feel welcome and at home. For events such as this, the more players that join, the more successful the event becomes!

GAME SETTINGS

All matches in this tournament will be played on 10-Wave Survival Mode on Hell on Earth difficulty using the Controlled Difficulty mod.


Matches played in this tournament will be harder than a typical vanilla Hell on Earth match, however not so difficult that an exemplary level of skill will be required. For example, there will be more Large ZEDs present than in a typical vanilla HoE game and teams will need to coordinate, communicate, and effectively use their combined resources to tackle the waves. In the end, this is more about how well you can work together as a team, not how good your mechanical skills are.

FORMAT

An open Group Stage for the first three weeks, leading into a seeded (via results from the Group Stage) Double Elimination bracket for the Playoffs.


The goal of each match is to earn the highest number of Points, which are earned through a unique scoring system created specifically for this tournament. The better your team performs, the more points you earn! In the Playoffs rounds, earning a higher score than the enemy team will win you the match and allow you to advance to the next round.

ANY OTHER CONSIDERATIONS?

A small level of commitment is minimally required to participate in this tournament.

Playing in this tournament is NOT meant to be a full time job, however you will still be expected to be there for your team and be able to commit a reasonable amount of time in a given week to practice, coordinate, and ensure your team's best chances of succeeding in each match.


Just because you have a full time job doesn't mean you aren't allowed to play in this tournament. We're simply asking for players to be willing to commit a small amount of time (within reason) to their teams.


Only players on the STEAM platform can sign up for this tournament.

Most of our underlying systems and servers depend on the player having a valid STEAMID64, and as such, it is not possible for us at this time to support players playing on the Epic Games platform. You will need a copy of the game on STEAM to be able to register and play.


We're very sorry about this and hope to be able to accommodate Epic players in the future!


Teams are filled via a draft selection in this tournament.

In a draft selection, team captains take turns selecting players until all players have been chosen.

To aid in producing balanced teams, all players will be placed into Experience Groups that denote a player's relative experience within CD.


This means that there is a (slight) chance that you may not get to play with your friends.

This is an international tournament.

Players from all regions are welcome to participate in this tournament, provided they meet the minimum criteria (outlined above). By signing up to play, ALL competitors are expected to understand that their teammates AND opponents may be based in a distant time zone, which means that some or all players in a team may be required to play at inopportune / abnormal times throughout the day.


Furthermore, ping may be a factor depending on where you are located in the world. There will be servers offered for matches in many of the major regions of the world, but pings within a team may differ due to the draft selection.
