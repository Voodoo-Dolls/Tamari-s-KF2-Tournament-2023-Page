Welcome to the Tamari's KF2 Summer Showdown 2023 CD Tournament!


This is a tournament for KILLING FLOOR 2 centered around the Controlled Difficulty mod -- a mod which allows for fine tuning of difficulty-specific game variables and obtaining a level of consistency and intensity that drives a team experience simply not available in the vanilla game.


The objective of this tournament is not only to unite the community, but to give players (especially newer ones) the opportunity to learn and grow alongside experienced players in mechanical skill, game knowledge, and confidence in a (friendly) competitive environment where there is no fear of being judged. If nothing else, the primary goal is to have FUN and make friends together!


This tournament is hosted by Tamari, Forrest Mark X, and the Tamari's KF2 Community and is (hopefully) one of many to come. There is no prize for winning, other than bragging rights, a whole lot of game knowledge, and maybe even some new friends to go with it!

Tamari's KF2

Tamari's KF2 is the result of the combined efforts of Tamari & Forrest Mark X to bring a modded experience to KF2 that refreshes the experience and enhances the game through quality-of-life features and changes, new gamemodes, and a chill and close-knit environment. We're a multi-continental KF2 community with over 29 servers supporting the NA and EU regions, and now also Southeast Asia / Australia as well!

Our Objective

Primarily to introduce people to higher levels of play (Controlled Difficulty), but the main objective above all else is FUN. We don't like drama and try our best to maintain a stress-free environment that feels more like a small gaming family than a group of friends playing games together.


Like playing standard Endless and Survival? We have something for that.

Like playing at difficulties harder than HoE? We have something for that.

Like playing custom, wacky, new modes? We have something for that as well.

There is something for everyone here and players of all types are welcome!


These are not your typical modded servers. The mods in use aim to enhance the vanilla experience as opposed to drastically changing it. As such, you can expect to find features that remedy some of the more clunky aspects of vanilla KF2 gameplay as well as features that allow players to customize their experience(s).

Highlights + Features

  • Quality of life features: Weapons never despawn, drop all weapons when you die, view dropped weapon information (owner, ammunition, etc), lock dropped weapons so that only the owner can pick them up, ping zeds/objects/items, ZED Time extension counter, improved Medic Buff HUD, see all teammates' healthbars directly on the HUD, and many many more..
  • Custom Gamemodes: Dozens of custom modes such as Arms Race, ZED Wars, King of the Hill, True Endless, Boss Rush, Time Attack, and many more that add some new flavor to the game
  • Discord-to-Server relay system: Users can chat between the KF2 servers and Discord and view a live feed of the game's current state from Discord (powered by my bot)
  • Server Achievements: 187 custom achievements spanning a variety of categories, all synced to an external database so that progress persists across all of our servers
  • Additional Prestige levels: 25 additional "Overload" Prestige Levels accessible after reaching maximum prestige. No statistical benefits are offered, just a new badge (and bragging rights)
  • Custom UI: Custom Chat Box and Console that support Emoji, color-coded text, and Icons, as well as a custom scoreboard that supports higher player counts and shows more information.
  • Client Customization: Players can change weapon glow colors, HUD colors, weapon FOV, toggle the ZED Time black/white filter, show Medic Buff timers on teammates, and tweak dozens of other clientside options
  • Cross Server Chat: Users can chat between servers using a command
  • Global Statistics: Track your total statistics across all servers such as Total Kills, Total Large Kills, Total Healing Done, etc.
